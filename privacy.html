Privacy Policy

The following describes how data is handled when you use this site.

Data collection

The app uses a static JSON file to provide artist data. No user-submitted personal information is required to use the helper. The site does not transmit game data or guesses to any external server as part of normal operation.

Analytics & third parties

Optional analytics or advertising scripts may be present in the future. These are provided by third parties and are subject to their own policies. Use a script blocker or browser privacy settings to avoid those. The site does not collect any information.
